â–¶ Effects of shifting speaker embeddings along each principal direction

The audio samples provided demonstrate how shifting speaker embeddings along different principal directions changes the resulting voices. You'll hear that VITS-generated voices exhibit noticeable timbre changes across most of the top 16 principal directions. In contrast, voices generated by the two ECAPA-TDNN models show much less change when shifting embeddings along principal directions beyond the 10th. You'll also hear that for ECAPA-TDNN (NANSY), only the first three principal directions affect pitch. But for ECAPA-TDNN (SV), many directions change the pitch, and not always in order. For example, the provided female voice changes pitch along directions 1, 3, 4, 5, 7, 9, 10, and 13. The male voice, on the other hand, changes pitch along directions 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 10, 11, 12, and 15.
